Argument Mirror

See your argument from both sides

Argument Mirror is a web tool designed to help you build empathy, strengthen critical thinking, and improve debate skills. By exploring both sides of your opinion, you gain perspective, reduce bias, and enhance your reasoning.

How It Works

  1. Your View: Start by sharing your opinion or stance on a topic.
  2. Role Reversal: Write a counter-argument as if you believed the opposite.
  3. See Both Sides: Compare your arguments side by side.
  4. Insight: Get feedback on bias, tone, and detail. Download or reflect further.

Why Use Argument Mirror?

  • Prepare for debates and essays
  • Improve conflict resolution skills
  • Encourage open-minded, logical thinking
